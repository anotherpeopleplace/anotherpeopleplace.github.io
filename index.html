The story so far...

In late 2023 we had the idea to start building a soundsystem. Over the course of last year we spent whatever time we could find, drawing up plans, self-doubting over design choices, making a mess with wood glue in the workshop, and painting by headtorch. After a few successful test outings in the last few months we're now buzzing to start a regular party using it. These parties will be held in Civic House's downstairs venue space - a building we're very familiar with through the running of Coorie Doon since 2023. This night aims to share a lot of the core ethos around the joy of dancing and community spirit but with an additional focus on the DIY exploration of audio and with a marginally 'deeper' sound, specifically revolving around the sounds of Detroit.

What to expect from these nights?

  • Two stacks of carefully tuned speakers featuring classic Altec designs (shout out to Cosmic Slop for the inspiration <3) powered by modern drivers and amplification
  • Sonic exploration through the music selections and the building/upgrading of the system over time
  • An, as yet, untested wooden parquet dancefloor that we drove across the country in a van to pick up
  • Some cool lights
  • Deep selections, big grooves, warm atmosphere, communal energy

House rules

  • Show kindness and respect towards fellow dancers
  • No photos, videos, or excessive chatting on the dancefloor
  • Zero tolerance for any discrimonatory language or actions, the dancefloor is a safe space for freedom of expression
